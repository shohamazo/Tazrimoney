# Firebase Studio

This is a NextJS starter in Firebase Studio.

To get started, take a look at src/app/page.tsx.

## Testing Your Application

You can easily test your web application on desktop and mobile devices. Use the preview URL provided in Firebase Studio.

### Web/Desktop Testing

1.  Open the preview URL in any web browser on your computer (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
2.  Interact with your application as any user would.

### iOS Testing (iPhone/iPad)

1.  Open the **Safari** app on your iOS device.
2.  Navigate to your application's preview URL.
3.  **To add it to your Home Screen (for an app-like experience):**
    *   Tap the **Share** button (a square with an arrow pointing up).
    *   Scroll down the share menu and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
    *   Confirm the name and tap **Add**. The app icon will now appear on your home screen.

### Android Testing

1.  Open the **Chrome** app on your Android device.
2.  Navigate to your application's preview URL.
3.  **To add it to your Home Screen (for an app-like experience):**
    *   Tap the **three-dot menu** icon in the top-right corner.
    *   Tap on **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.
    *   Follow the on-screen prompts to install. The app icon will now appear on your home screen or in your app drawer.
